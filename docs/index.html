Andaleeb Rahman

I am an applied economist at the Tata-Cornell Institute for Agriculture and Nutrition (TCI), Cornell University, Ithaca, NY. My research lies broadly in the area of developmental impact evaluation with a focus on food security, nutrition, agriculture, social protection, and political economy.

Contact: Google Scholar | LinkedIn | Email: ar687@cornell.edu
